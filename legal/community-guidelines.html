Talor Community Guidelines

Effective date: March 3, 2026 · Contact: support@talor.dev

1. Respect First

Treat everyone with dignity. Harassment, hate speech, threats, bullying, and discrimination are not allowed.

2. Authenticity

Do not impersonate others or misrepresent your identity, work history, or outcomes.

3. Safe Content

Do not post illegal, sexually exploitative, violent-extremist, or otherwise harmful content.

4. Honest Marketplace Behavior

  • Describe Works accurately.
  • Do not manipulate ratings, reviews, or transactions.
  • No spam, scams, or deceptive promotions.

5. IP Respect

Only post content you own or are authorized to use.

6. Enforcement

Talor may remove content, limit visibility, issue warnings, suspend, or terminate accounts for violations.
