What is Talor?

Talor is a social marketplace where ordinary people can be seen for what they can actually do. If life pushed your passion to the side, Talor helps you bring it back into your daily reality with a practical path from sharing to earning.

You do not need a huge audience, fancy branding, or a perfect profile to begin. You just need one real talent, one clear work, and one moment of courage to put it out there.
