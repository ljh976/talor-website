What is Talor?

Talor brings social discovery and simple buying together in one place, so people are seen for what they can actually do instead of how loud they are online. Whether you are returning to an old passion, testing a side skill after work, or finally sharing something you have kept to yourself, Talor helps you show it clearly and turn it into opportunity.

At its core, Talor is built on a simple belief: everyone has talent, but everyday life often pushes dreams to the background. Talor gives ordinary people a practical way to start again—post Works, build trust step by step, find people who value what you do, and grow confidence as your talent creates real momentum.
